Expedition Comparison Matrix

A definitive side-by-side evaluation to help your family decide.

Bali Retreat

Oct 2 - 7 Oct 15 - 20

Estimated Per Pax Cost

Premium (5-Star + SQ Flights) ~₹1.48L
Budget (4-Star + AirAsia) ~₹93,000

Scenery & Vibe

Lush tropical jungles, volcanic beaches, and deeply spiritual Hindu culture. The vibe is highly relaxing, focused on luxury resorts, beach clubs, and serene temples.

Core Activities

  • Glass-bottom Catamaran Tour
  • Uluwatu Sunset Kecak Fire Dance
  • Tegalalang Rice Terraces Walk
  • Waterbom Bali Waterpark (Lazy River)

Pros & Cons

  • World-class, large-scale luxury resorts perfect for large groups.
  • Better dedicated entertainment for kids (like Waterbom Bali).
  • Longer travel time from Jaipur (~12-14 hours with layover).
  • Higher overall budget compared to Sri Lanka.

Sri Lanka Heritage

Oct 2 - 7 Oct 15 - 20

Estimated Per Pax Cost

Premium (5-Star + AI Flights) ~₹1.33L
Budget (4-Star + IndiGo) ~₹71,000

Scenery & Vibe

Colonial Dutch architecture, expansive Indian Ocean coastlines, and lush mangroves. The vibe is historical, culturally immersive, and extremely laid back.

Core Activities

  • Bentota Coastline Relaxation
  • Madu River Mangrove Safari
  • Galle Dutch Fort Heritage Walk
  • Colombo City Tour & Shopping

Pros & Cons

  • Significantly cheaper, especially in the budget tier.
  • Shorter travel time from India (8-10 hours with a quick layover).
  • Less "mega-resort" infrastructure compared to Bali.
  • Activities are more historical/nature-based rather than theme parks.
